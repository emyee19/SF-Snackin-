Dessert and Drinks

Golden Island, a popular Hong Kong dessert Cafe, is the prime hangout place for high schoolers and old asian people on late Friday and Saturday nights. Known for their fruit sagos and hot desserts, this place is a must try! Caution: Service is slow and wait time tends to be around an hour around late afternoon. Location: 1300 Noriega St, San Francisco, CA 94122
Powder is a shaved ice cream that offers a variety of base flavors such as black sesame, strawberry, matcha green tea, or vietnamese coffee. From there, you can choose to add toppings like mochi! mhmm... so good! Location: 260 Divisadero St., San Francisco, CA 94117
Bi-Rite Creamery is an San Francisco classic! On hot days, the line wraps around the block, but the wait is sooo worth it! Location: 3692 18th St, San Francisco, CA 94110
