Twin Peaks is one of the prettiest views of the city. It's a great scenic spot to watch the sunrise and sunset.
The Golden Gate Bridge is the symbolic icon that is the literal bridge-way to enter the city. This magnificent structure is one of the most popular tourist attractions. Bakers Beach, Fort Point, Lands End, and Crissy Fields are my favorite destinations to look at the bridge. Or just go walk across!!
